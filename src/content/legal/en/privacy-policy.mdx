## Privacy Policy

### 1. Data Controller

**Sens\*Hac**
Contact: [ester@senshac.com](mailto:ester@senshac.com)

### 2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

- **Contact Information**: Name, email address, phone number when you contact us through our forms.
- **Project Information**: Details about your project requirements and preferences.
- **Communication Data**: Records of correspondence when you contact us.

### 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Respond to your inquiries and provide customer service
- Communicate with you about projects and services
- Send you updates and marketing communications (with your consent)
- Improve our website and services

### 4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

- **Consent**: When you voluntarily provide your data through our contact forms
- **Legitimate Interest**: To respond to inquiries and improve our services
- **Contractual Necessity**: When processing is necessary for project execution

### 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

- Service providers who assist in our operations
- Professional advisors (lawyers, accountants)
- Authorities when required by law

### 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, typically:

- Contact form data: 2 years from last contact
- Project data: 7 years after project completion (legal requirements)

### 7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Request erasure of your data
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
- Object to processing
- Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at [ester@senshac.com](mailto:ester@senshac.com).

### 8. International Transfers

Your data is primarily processed within the European Economic Area (EEA). If transferred outside the EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

## Cookie-Free Website

This website **does not use cookies**. We respect your privacy and have designed our site to function without storing information on your device.

### Cookie-Free Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This tool:

- **Does not use cookies** or store any data on your device
- **Does not track users** individually across sessions
- **Does not collect personally identifiable information**
- Only records aggregated, anonymous data about page visits

### No Third-Party Tracking

We do not integrate third-party services that set tracking cookies. There are no:

- Google Analytics cookies
- Social media cookies
- Advertising or remarketing cookies

### Browser Storage

We use browser local storage only for:

- Remembering your language preferences

This information stays only on your device and is never sent to our servers.
